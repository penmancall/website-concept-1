About Me.

My name is Callum, I am an industrial design graduate on the lookout for career opportunities. I studied at Victoria University of Wellington, and completed a bachelor of design innovation majoring in industrial design in October 2017.

My experience includes three years of experience using CAD packages such as Solidworks and Rhinoceros to design for additive manufacturing technologies as well as other CNC machining techniques. I have a keen eye for attention to detail regarding design. I take a pragmatic approach to problems, and attempt to arrive at a solution which does not sacrifice usability or complicate the final solution simply to add aesthetic value.

Some practical attributes I have include hands on experience operating consumer level 3D printers such as the UP! Box FDM machines, as well as designing parts to be made on printers such as the Stratasys J750 and Connex printers. I also have experience using rendering software such as Keyshot, to produce images of prototypes and working animations to demonstrate features of a design.

As a hobby, I am also interested in front end web design, and I have around 6 months experience using HTML, CSS + Bootstrap and jQuery. Currently my main interest in this field is responsive web design, and creating a unique user experience which works on computers and mobile devices. I also have experience using Arduino & Raspberry Pi, using sensors and creating interactive projects. I have basic knowledge of C/C++ and Python.
